About Us

SHAVAK is one among the best and trusted website which gives you information about the tourist and traveller services in your locality. . We believe in our ideas to help the people by providing the information about the travel services available in their locality

SHAVAK started With aim to providing information about E-Rickshaw services near Rajkiya Engineering College Bijnor as there are unavailability of E-Rickshaws sometime near the college

Many students in the college comes from the different cities of the country.If they want to go their home or coming from the home they find difficulties to find E-Rickshaws from station to college . This website started with a purpose of providing the information about the E-Rickshaw drivers which provides their services nearby college.It also includes the information about the van drivers near the college and also includes information about the buses which operates from the Chandpur to the different cities of the country.

Our aim is to enlist the information about the van drivers, Rickshaw drivers and buses of the different part of the country so that anyone can contact the services nearby their locality in case of Emergency to help. We focus on providing trustable information to help the people across different part of th country.
